Browse by subject

The Freshwater Science program draws from these UBCO subjects (codes in the format the calendar uses):

  • FWSC_O — Freshwater Science (program-specific courses)
  • BIOL_O — Biology
  • EESC_O — Earth and Environmental Sciences
  • CHEM_O — Chemistry
  • GEOG_O — Geography
  • MATH_O — Mathematics
  • PHYS_O — Physics
  • STAT_O — Statistics
  • INDG_O — Indigenous Studies
  • ENGL_O — English

When the scraper is wired up, this page will render a sortable table with: course code, title, credits, lecture-lab pattern, prerequisites, equivalencies, and an “offered next academic year?” badge.

Year structure (Freshwater Science Major)

Year 1 (30 credits)

  • BIOL_O 116 and 125 (6)
  • CHEM_O 121 and 123 (6)
  • EESC_O 111 and 112 (6)
  • MATH_O 100 (3) plus one of MATH_O 101 or 103 (3)
  • One of PHYS_O 111 or 112 (3) plus one of PHYS_O 121 or 122 (3)

Year 2 (30 credits)

  • BIOL_O 201 (3)
  • One of BIOL_O 202, GEOG_O 271, STAT_O 121, STAT_O 230 (3)
  • One of CHEM_O 201 or 210 (3) plus CHEM_O 211 (3)
  • EESC_O 205 and 222 (6)
  • English / communication electives (6)
  • INDG_O 100 or ENGL_O 114 (3)
  • Non-science electives (3)

Years 3 and 4 (60 credits)

  • BIOL_O 308 (3)
  • FWSC_O 375 (3)
  • CHEM_O 301 (3)
  • EESC_O 212, 301, 402 (9)
  • One of EESC_O 342, 413, 435 (3)
  • Two of EESC_O 309, 323, 423 (6)
  • One of EESC_O 313, 314, 315 (3)
  • Upper-level science electives (9)
  • Non-science electives (6)
  • General electives (15)

Honours

The Honours track requires a 76% minimum grade average in 24 credits from designated upper-level courses plus EESC_O 449 (Honours Thesis, 6 credits).
