Map of how UBC Okanagan's **Freshwater Science (BSc)** is structured,
and how it complements **Chemistry, Biology,** and **Engineering**
pathways.

**Freshwater Science: what the pathway looks like**

- **Degree options & co-op.** Major and Honours are offered, with co-op
  available. The program is intentionally interdisciplinary and draws on
  biology, chemistry, geography, and earth & environmental sciences.
  [UBC Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)

- **Typical coursework.** Early science fundamentals (BIOL 116/201, CHEM
  211/301, EESC 101/111/205/222) lead into upper-level limnology,
  hydrogeology, global biogeochemistry, freshwater resources, and
  applied hydrology/geomorphology (e.g., EESC 301, 342, 402, 413, 435).
  [UBC Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)

- **Honours & research.** Honours requires EESC 449 (thesis) and high
  standing; eligible upper-level credits explicitly span BIOL, CHEM,
  EESC, FWSC, GEOG, and GISC---underscoring the cross-disciplinary
  design. [UBC Academic
  Calendar](https://okanagan.calendar.ubc.ca/faculties-schools-and-colleges/faculty-science/bachelor-science-programs/major-programs/freshwater-science)

- **Structured "program plan."** UBCO's official Freshwater Science
  Program Plan highlights experiential learning (co-op, field courses),
  research, Go Global, and step-by-step milestone planning from first
  year to graduation. [UBC Okanagan
  Students](https://students.ok.ubc.ca/wp-content/uploads/sites/90/2025/06/BSc-Freshwater-Science_Program-Plan.pdf)

**How it complements Chemistry**

- **Aquatic/Environmental chemistry focus.** Freshwater Science requires
  **CHEM 301 (Aqueous Environmental Chemistry)** and **CHEM 211
  (Analytical Chemistry)**---a natural bridge into Chemistry's
  analytical/environmental strengths and instrumentation (e.g., FiLTER
  lab). [UBC Undergraduate
  Admissions+1](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)

- **Program synergies.** Chemistry at UBCO offers a range of
  environmental and green chemistry options, supporting extensive
  undergraduate research that is particularly useful for projects on
  contaminants, redox/speciation, and trace analysis in waters.
  [chemistry.ok.ubc.ca](https://chemistry.ok.ubc.ca/undergraduate/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)[UBC
  Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/chemistry-okanagan/)

- **Suggested bundle.** Pair **EESC 301/309/423**, **CHEM 211/301**, and
  a **GISC Minor** to build a "water quality & biogeochemistry" pathway
  with spatial analysis skills. [UBC Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)[eesc.ok.ubc.ca](https://eesc.ok.ubc.ca/undergraduate/gisc-minor/)

**How it complements Biology**

- **Aquatic ecology & biomonitoring.** Core Biology inside the
  Freshwater pathway includes **BIOL 201** and **BIOL 375 (Flora & Fauna
  of Inland Waters)**, aligning with Biology's ecology/conservation
  strengths and field-based learning (BRAES institute). [UBC
  Undergraduate
  Admissions+1](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)

- **Program synergies.** UBCO Biology offers co-op, honours, and
  extensive field options---great for limnology, population ecology, and
  biodiversity applications in lakes and streams. [UBC Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/biology-okanagan/)

- **Suggested bundle.** Combine **EESC 301/402**, **BIOL 308/375**, and
  directed studies/honours to create an "aquatic ecology & conservation"
  pathway (with co-op placements in government/consulting). [UBC
  Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)

**How it complements Engineering (Civil/Environmental focus)**

- **Hydrology & hydraulics interface.** Freshwater Science covers
  hydrology (EESC 205/413), hydrogeology (EESC 342), and fluvial field
  techniques (EESC 435), which complement Civil Engineering themes like
  open-channel flow and water management systems. [UBC Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)[School
  of
  Engineering](https://engineering.ok.ubc.ca/programs-admissions/civil/)

- **Program synergies.** UBCO Civil Engineering features
  water-environment research, **ENGR 342 Open Channel Flow**, and an
  **Environmental Engineering** concentration---useful pairings for
  students interested in watershed modelling, flood/drought risk, and
  water infrastructure. [School of
  Engineering](https://engineering.ok.ubc.ca/programs-admissions/civil/)

- **Suggested bundle.** A "hydrology & water resources" package: **EESC
  413/435/423**, **EESC 342**, plus **ENGR 342** (where eligible) and a
  **GISC Minor** for geospatial modelling. (Note: access to ENGR courses
  depends on prerequisites/seat availability; plan with advisors.) [UBC
  Undergraduate
  Admissions](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)[School
  of
  Engineering](https://engineering.ok.ubc.ca/programs-admissions/civil/)[eesc.ok.ubc.ca](https://eesc.ok.ubc.ca/undergraduate/gisc-minor/)

**Practical notes for mixing & matching**

- **Co-op across programs.** Co-op is available in Freshwater,
  Chemistry, Biology, and Engineering---ideal for stitching together
  applied experiences (e.g., labs, consulting, utilities). [UBC
  Undergraduate Admissions+2UBC Undergraduate
  Admissions+2](https://you.ubc.ca/ubc_programs/freshwater-science/)[School
  of
  Engineering](https://engineering.ok.ubc.ca/programs-admissions/civil/)

- **Credit double-counting.** The calendar caps double-counting of
  **upper-level** credits toward a second major/minor (generally max 6
  credits for program-specified requirements), so schedule
  intentionally. [UBC Academic
  Calendar](https://okanagan.calendar.ubc.ca/faculties-schools-and-colleges/faculty-science/bachelor-science-programs/major-programs/freshwater-science)

- **Advising & plans.** Use the official **Program Plan** and meet
  program advisors early---especially if you're eyeing Honours, co-op,
  or cross-faculty electives. [UBC Okanagan
  Students](https://students.ok.ubc.ca/wp-content/uploads/sites/90/2025/06/BSc-Freshwater-Science_Program-Plan.pdf)
