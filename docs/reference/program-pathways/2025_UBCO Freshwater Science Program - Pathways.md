# UBCO Freshwater Science Program Pathway

## Course Offerings for 2025 Winter Session - Term 1 (Fall 2025)

This document provides a visual and descriptive overview of the course
progression for the Bachelor of Science in Freshwater Science at the
University of British Columbia Okanagan (UBCO). The pathway highlights
the courses scheduled to be offered in Term 1 of the 2025 Winter
Session.

### Course Pathway Visualization

The following diagram illustrates the four-year plan for the Freshwater
Science program. Courses are organized by year, and arrows indicate
prerequisite relationships.

- **Green Boxes**: Courses offered in 2025W Term 1.

- **Grey Boxes**: Courses not offered in 2025W Term 1.

- **Ovals**: Indicate elective course choices.
