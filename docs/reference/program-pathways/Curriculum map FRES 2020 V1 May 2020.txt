Suggested Program  choices

The information in the graphic has been put together to provide some guidance to Freshwater Science Majors as to progression through the program which will meet requirements of the major.  Please read the notes under each year for further information.  This advice is not comprehensive and there are many possible options.  Direct discussions with Academic Advising and the Freshwater Science Program Advisor are always the best option for determining your final program choices.   Some students choose to investigate the possibility for registration as a professional geoscientist,  agrologist , biologist or environmental professional after graduation.  These options may require careful planning, and may involve taking additional courses.  Advice as early as possible from the Freshwater Science program advisor is recommended.
